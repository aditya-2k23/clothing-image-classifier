Tips for Best Results

Follow these simple tips to get the most accurate predictions:

Single Item Only

Upload a photo with just one clothing item. Place it on a flat surface rather than wearing it.

Plain Background

Use a solid white or black background. Avoid patterns or busy surfaces.

Good Lighting

Make sure the item is well-lit and clearly visible. Avoid dark or overexposed photos.

Center the Item

Position the clothing item in the center and fill most of the frame.
